

# 🏠 Complete House Vocabulary Course (Beginner → Advanced)

Think of this as a roadmap to reach **C1/C2-level vocabulary** related to homes, daily life, maintenance, construction, and interior design. Learn one module at a time.

---

# Level 1 — Foundation (A1)

**Goal:** Talk about your home and common objects.

## Rooms

* house, home, apartment, flat
* bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, living room
* dining room, hallway, balcony, garage

## Furniture
      
* bed, chair, table, sofa, desk
* cupboard, shelf, cabinet
* mirror, wardrobe

## Objects

* door, window, wall, floor, ceiling
* key, lock, switch
* curtain, mat, rug

### Practice

> My apartment has two bedrooms and one kitchen. There is a sofa in the living room.
