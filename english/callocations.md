Learning collocations is one of the fastest ways to sound natural in English. Native speakers don't build sentences word-by-word; they use word combinations (collocations) automatically.

Examples:

* **Make a decision** ✅ (not *do a decision*)
* **Heavy rain** ✅ (not *strong rain*)
* **Take a break** ✅ (not *do a break*)

### How to Learn These Efficiently

For each collocation:

1. Learn the meaning.
2. Create 3 sentences about your life.
3. Use it in speaking at work.
4. Review 20 collocations daily.
5. Keep a notebook of examples.

For your goal of improving workplace communication at Walmart and becoming more fluent in meetings, focus first on collocations **101–300**, because these are the ones most commonly used in professional conversations, emails, presentations, and technical discussions.
